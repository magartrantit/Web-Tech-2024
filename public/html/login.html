About our project - Culinary Preferences Organizer

The Culinary Preferences Organizer is a web application that manages culinary preferences using data from Open Food Facts and a custom REST/GraphQL API. It handles categories, prices, ingredients, restrictions, and more. It also supports shopping list creation, user management, and exports statistics in CSV and PDF formats.
